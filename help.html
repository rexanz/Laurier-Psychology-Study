Help

F.A.Q.

  • Question: How do I find the class that I'm looking for?

  • Answer: Navigate to the menu on the top right of the page and click class list. Once there select the year your class is in. After you should be greeted with all the available classes for that year. Find the one you are looking for and click it and let the learning begin.


  • Question: Why is the video not playing?

  • Answer: The video may not be playing for many reasons. First, try refreshing your browser. If the video still does not play, right click the video and select 'copy video url'. Paste that link into the search bar at the top of your browser and watch it on youtube.com. If this still does not work, feel free to contact us on our twitter via direct message about your problem.
